Executes a function synchronoulsy with timeout control The function may or may not return, and may or may not receive parameters.

Template Parameters
t_functionthe function to be executed. It may not return, as

void t_function(std::function<bool()> p_timeout, t_params... p_params) or it may return, as in

some-ret t_function(std::function<bool()> p_timeout, t_params... p_params)

where p_timeout informs that the function has exceeded the maximum time of execution, and can stop executing

So, t_function should eventually call for p_timeout() to decide if it must continue to exec_sync

Template Parameters
t_paramspossible parameters of t_function
Parameters
p_max_timeis the maximum amount of time that p_function should take to exec_sync
p_functionis a function with one of the signatures described above
p_paramsare the possible arguments for p_function
Attention
Please, take a look at tnct/async/exp/executer_000/main.cpp for examples
